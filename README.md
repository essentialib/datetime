# date

### format specifiers
| Format | Description                             | Example                 |
|--------|-----------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| `s`    | The seconds of the minute between 0-59. | "0" to "59"             |
| `ss`   | The seconds of the minute between 0-59. | "00" to "59"            |
| `m`    | The minute of the hour between 0-59.    | "0" to "59"             |
| `mm`   | The minute of the hour between 0-59.    | "00" to "59"            |
| `h`    | The hour of the day between 1-12.       | "1" to "12"             |
| `hh`   | The hour of the day between 1-12.       | "01" to "12"            |
| `i`    | The hour of the day between 0-23.       | "0" to "23"             |
| `ii`   | The hour of the day between 0-23.       | "00" to "23"            |
| `t`    | The locale's AM/PM text.                | "AM" or "PM"            |
| `D`    | The day of the month between 1 and 31.  | "1" to "31"             |
| `DD`   | The day of the month between 1 and 31.  | "01" to "31"            |
| `W`    | The abbreviated day of the week.        | "Sun" to "Sat"          |
| `WW`   | The full day of the week.               | "Sunday" to "Saturday"  |
| `M`    | The month of the year between 1-12.     | "1" to "12"             |
| `MM`   | The month of the year between 1-12.     | "01" to "12"            |
| `MMM`  | The abbreviated month name.             | "Jan" to "Dec"          |
| `MMMM` | The full month name.                    | "January" to "December" |
| `Y`    | The year without century.               | "95" or "06"            |
| `YY`   | The year with century.                  | "1995" or "2006"        |
